Introduction

The Air Force Research Lab Information Directorate’s Basic Research for Artificial Intelligence (BRAIN) Team is a group of in-house researchers that develop novel machine learning algorithms to address unique Air Force challenges related to Artificial Intelligence. Of particular interest to the BRAIN Team are topics related to:

  • Data Efficiency
  • Data Representations
  • Model Evaluation
  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Robustness
  • Self Supervision

Look around this webpage to learn about the team’s past and ongoing projects, publications, team members, and publicly available datasets. Please contact individual team members if you are interested in a particular project or dataset!
